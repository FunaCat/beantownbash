About Us

The objective of the Amherst-Pelham Regional High School athletic program is to have a well-rounded athletic program. Core values such as fair play, loyalty, teamwork, and determination give our school a sense of community. We encourage students to participate in sports to develop lasting friendships and an understanding of school pride. Students participating in different types of activities gain a variety of experiences that make worthy contributions to ARHS. The school provides equal opportunity for all students to participate in intramural and interscholastic sports.
